If you are traveling from out of town, we always recommend flying into Burbank Airport. If you fly into LAX, consider taking the FlyAway bus, which drops you off at Union Station in downtown LA.

Our events are centered around the East Side of LA (we live in Echo Park!) â€“ so all of our events will be possible to use ride shares/bus to get around, but LA is a car-based city, so renting a car is definitely an option if you want to get to new/different neighborhoods.
Our events are centered around East Los Angeles, so we recommend staying at an Airbnb or hotel near Echo Park, Downtown, Silverlake, etc.

Hotel Room Block: We have a room block with discounted rates at the VERY funky Westin Bonaventure in Downtown LA ~20 mins from all venues, where countless famous movies have been shot, as well as this insane Fenty fashion show. They have a 360 rotating bar at the top, not to be missed, unless you get nauseous easily. You can book rooms here [must be booked by May 1st].

Other options include the Silver Lake Pool and Inn (walking distance to the event on Saturday) and AirBnbs!
